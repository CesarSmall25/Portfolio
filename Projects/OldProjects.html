Summary

These are some of the older projects I've worked on, either while I was in school or in my spare time. Some of these projects are no longer being actively developed, but they still showcase my skills and experience as a developer. Feel free to explore each project to learn more about them. They can also be found on my GitHub.

School Projects

These are placed in order from oldest to newest.
  • Number Guessing Game - My first ever game. This was a simple number guessing game with varying levels of difficulty.
  • Tic Tac Toe - A simple tic tac toe game I made for my c class. One of the first projects I worked on.
  • Class Grade App - An application I developed to help students calculate their grades based on assignments and exams.
  • Lab Shooter - My first actual game made with a commercial engine. Made in Unity, this was a VR game that taught me my initial understanding about game development and VR mechanics.
  • Preyed Upon - My final year project, this was a horror VR game that was developed with 2 other classmates. I wrote the scripts and game logic.
